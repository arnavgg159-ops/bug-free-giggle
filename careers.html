Careers in Artificial Intelligence

```

Artificial Intelligence is creating exciting career opportunities. Students with strong technical and problem-solving skills can pursue a variety of professions in the AI industry.

```
```

AI Engineer

AI Engineers develop intelligent systems, applications, and software that can perform tasks autonomously.

Data Scientist

Data Scientists analyze large datasets to discover patterns and help organizations make informed decisions.

Machine Learning Engineer

These professionals design and train machine learning models that improve through experience and data.

Robotics Engineer

Robotics Engineers build intelligent machines capable of performing tasks in industries and daily life.

Cybersecurity Specialist

AI is increasingly used in cybersecurity to detect threats, prevent attacks, and improve digital safety.

AI Researcher

AI Researchers explore new methods and technologies to advance the capabilities of intelligent systems.
```
