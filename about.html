What is Artificial Intelligence?

```

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

```
```

Definition

AI refers to systems capable of simulating human intelligence to perform complex tasks efficiently.

History of AI

The concept of AI emerged in the 1950s and has evolved rapidly with advancements in computing power and data availability.

Types of AI

AI can be categorized into Narrow AI, General AI, and Super AI based on capability and intelligence level.
```
